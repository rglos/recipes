# Egg Foo Young

## Foo Yung Sauce (optional)

- 125 ml soup stock
- 1/2 (2 ml) teaspoon sugar
- 2 (10 ml) teaspoons soy sauce
- 1/4 (1 ml) teaspoon sesame oil
- 1 1/2 (7 ml) teaspoons cornstarch solution

## Omelet

- 3-4 large eggs, lightly beaten
- 1 cup (250 ml) bean sprouts
- 2 tablespoons chopped BBQ pork (optional)
- 1 cup (250 ml) chopped cooked ham
- 1 cup (250 ml) fresh or cooked shrimp
- 2 stalks green onions, chopped
- 2 tablespoons (30 ml) peas and carrots
- 1/2 teaspoon (2 ml) sugar
- dash of white pepper
- 1 tablespoon (15 ml) flour
- salt to taste
