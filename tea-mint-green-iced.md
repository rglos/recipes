# Mint Iced Tea

## Brew tea

- [Smith Tea -Mediterranean Mint Iced Tea – Smith Teamaker](https://www.smithtea.com/products/mediterranean-mint-iced-tea)

Put 2 bags in a half gallon jar, fill with water, set on patio with sunlight for the day

## Make the simple syrup

- 240g (1 cup) water
- 200g (1 cup) sugar
- 1-2 cups fresh mint leaves

In a small saucepan, combine the water and sugar. Bring to a simmer, stirring until the sugar is dissolved. Remove from heat and add the mint leaves. Let steep for 15-20 minutes, then strain out the mint leaves.

## Combine and serve

 - 240g of brewed tea
 - 30g of simple syrup
 - glass full of ice
 - garnish with fresh mint leaves
