# Cornbread

## Ingredients

- 4 tablespoons (1/2 stick) unsalted butter, melted, plus additional for greasing the pan
- Dry
  - 1 cup (120g) flour
  - 1 cup (120g) cornmeal
  - 1 tablespoon baking powder
  - 1 teaspoon salt
- Wet
  - 2 eggs
  - 1 cup (240g) buttermilk (I use yogurt)
  - 1/4 (60g) cup maple syrup

## Directions

- Preheat oven to 425.  Lightly grease an 8-inch square or 9-inch round baking pan with butter.  I use a cast iron skillet.
- In medium bowl combine dry ingredients and whisk together,
- In large bowl, break eggs and beat them with whisk.  Add yogurt and syrup and whisk until uniform.
- Add dry to wet and mix together so that dry is incorporated, don't over mix.  Pour in melted butter and incorporate again.
- Pour batter into prepared skillet,
- Bake at 425 for 20 to 25 minutes.  I go 25 minutes.  Should be lightly browned, pulling away from sides of skillet, test with a toothpick to make sure comes out clean in center.
