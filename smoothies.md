# Smoothies

## Chocolate Peanut Butter 
- 1/2 cup (120ml) milk of choice
- 1 banana 
- 1 tablespoon (14g) cocoa powder
- 2 tablespoons (40g) peanut butter
- 3 pitted dates
- handful of crushed ice
