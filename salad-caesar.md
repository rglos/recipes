# Caesar Salad

## Tasting History Version

### Croutons

- 2 cups of day old italian bread
- 2 cloves crushed garlic
- 3-4 tablespoons olive oil


### Dressing

- 2 cloves crushed garlic
- 2/3 cup (160g) of olive oil
- 1/4 cup (25g) parmesan cheese
- 2 medium heads of romaine lettuce
- 1/2 teaspoon kosher salt
- 1 egg yolk
- fresh ground black pepper
- juice of 1 lime (30g) - yes LIME not LEMON
- 2 tablespoons of worcester Sauce

### Directions

Don't fret the ingredients.  Add or measure as needed.

1. Romaine, cut and wash romaine, need about 20 leaves, put in frig for about 1 hour.  So get dry and chilled.
2. Croutons - in large bowl mix garlic with olive oil, add pinch of salt and crack of pepper, add bread and toss - put on sheet tray and bake at 375 for 15-20 minutes
3. Dressing - add garlic, worcester, pepper, egg yolk, lime juice, salt.  Stir.  While stirring, drizzle in olive oil slowly.  Add parmesan cheese.  Add romaine to coat them as large uncut leaves.  Or forget that and cut them up.
