# Iced Matcha Tea

## Directions

Boil some water to bloom the matcha.

Blend it all together in the Vitamix.

| ingredient / notes | 1 person | 2 people | 3 people |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Matcha Green Tea | 1 teaspoon | 2 teaspoons | 1 tablespoon |
| Water | 30 grams | 60 grams | 90 grams |
| Mix in container and let bloom for 1-2 minutes |
| Vitamix |
| Milk or Almond Milk | 240 grams | 480 grams | 720 grams |
| Honey (or sweetner of choice) | 30 grams | 60 grams | 90 grams |
| Pour over glass full of ice |

Don't put the ice in the Vitamix for this recipe, it causes the matcha to stick to the cubes and get clumpy.  Just pour it over ice in a glass after it's all mixed.
